Transcendence Mods


Playership Bones & Ghosts

Playership Bones & Ghosts (2016) is a mod that imports the "bones" feature from NetHack. Playerships from previous games have a chance of showing up as wrecks (with all their items) or "ghosts" which wander the system as a hostile. When a player gets destroyed, this mod calls the previously unused unvGetExtensionData and unvSetExtensionData functions to store detailed data about the player's configuration at the time of destruction


Command Screen

Command Screen (2017) is an advanced mod that exploits the DockScreen's canvas functionality to the max. Use the Command Screen to get an abstract view of all ships and stations in the system, represented as colored blocks. You can pan across and zoom in on the map; inspect ships and stations in detail; and select and give orders to ships under your control.


Commonwealth Police

Commonwealth Police (2018) is a GTA-inspired mod that turns up the firepower against players who commit piracy and destroy outbound ships. Now each station has its own police captain to patrol around, respond to hostiles, confiscate cargo crates, and scuttle wrecks. If the player has an active criminal record, a squad of police ships surrounds the player and orders them to dock at the nearest Commonwealth station for trial. Stations also become angry at the player until all weapons are disabled. Refusing to comply can lead to military action.


Recycler Nomads

Recycler Nomads are ships that wander around and loot wrecks. They convert items into useful enhancers, which they sell at a premium.


Arch Globals

Arch Globals is a library of miscellaneous TLisp functions written for the development of my mods.
