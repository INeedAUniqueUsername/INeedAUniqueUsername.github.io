ASECII

Introduction

ASECII (ASCII Sprite Editing Console II) is a fully featured ASCII art painter inspired by Aseprite and REXPaint. ASECII supports rect / wand / lasso selection, moving, cut & paste, palettes, layers, saving / loading, etc.
