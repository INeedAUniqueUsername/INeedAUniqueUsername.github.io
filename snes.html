Super Nostalgia Entertainment Syndrome

Introduction

Super Nostalgia Entertainment Syndrome is a fast-paced endless-survival space game. Fly a small starship and face off against waves of enemy starships. Fire long-range lasers from a manually-aimed cannon or mouse-controlled turret, or aim your starship's plasma exhaust trail at foes for a deadly close-range attack. Be careful not to crash into other starships!

Lost in Space

You are stuck in the Zone of No Escape. No matter how fast you are, wraparound prevents you from leaving. Spawning around you are unidentified starships that approach you aggressively, responding to your transmissions with radio silence. The hostile ships only get faster and stronger over time. Your ship sensors become blurry as the internal systems are damaged. How long can you survive?
